Index framework

How AI “sees” a website

AIVI analyzes four core layers of AI perception:

Structural Legibility

Can AI reliably parse, navigate, and segment your site?

Semantic Clarity

Does AI understand your offering — or fill in the gaps with assumptions?

Authority Recognition

Does AI treat your content as reference-worthy or disposable?

Representation Risk

How likely AI is to misstate, oversimplify, or exclude your brand when generating answers

Each layer contributes to your AI Visibility Index reading — a single, interpretable signal of how AI systems perceive your presence.

What AIVI is — and is not

Clarity

AIVI is an observational index. It measures how AI systems interpret and represent a website based on observable signals.

  • Not an SEO tool — no keywords, backlinks, or rank tracking
  • Not a content grader — it observes interpretability, not style preference
  • Not a prompt test — it evaluates persistent signals, not a single-model output
  • Not a checklist — it identifies stability and risk, not to-do lists
  • Not a guarantee engine — it does not promise outcomes, rankings, or citations
