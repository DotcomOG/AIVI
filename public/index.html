How AI “sees” a website

AIVI analyzes four core layers of AI perception. Each layer contributes to a single, interpretable Index reading that reflects how AI is likely to understand and represent your website today.

▣

Structural Legibility

Can AI reliably parse, navigate, and segment your site?

▣

Semantic Clarity

Does AI understand your offering, or fill in the gaps with assumptions?

▣

Authority Recognition

Does AI treat your content as reference-worthy, or disposable?

⚠︎

Representation Risk

How likely AI is to misstate, oversimplify, or exclude your brand when generating answers.

What AIVI is, and is not

AIVI is an observational index. It measures how AI systems interpret and represent a website based on observable signals.

What AIVI is

An Index reading that summarizes observable AI perception signals and provides a clear, interpretable view of how AI is likely to understand and represent your website.

What AIVI is not

Not an SEO tool. No keywords, backlinks, or rank tracking. Not a content grader. Not a prompt test. Not a checklist. Not a guarantee engine.

AIVI does not tell you what to do. It shows you what AI already believes.
