    What goes into your AiVi score?

    Your AiVi score is calculated across four core pillars:

    AI Access Readiness (25 points)
    How easily AI systems can discover, crawl, and understand your site structure.

    Trust & Verification Signals (25 points)
    Authority indicators, security implementation, and credibility markers that AI engines prioritize.

    LLM Interpretability & Clarity (25 points)
    Content organization, semantic markup, and clarity for AI language models.

    Prompt-Pattern Alignment (25 points)
    How well your content matches common query patterns and question-based discovery.

    Each factor is weighted based on its impact on AI discovery and visibility.
